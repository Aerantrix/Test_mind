❌ Doesn't work / strange

Timecode What happened Severity
~10:00 A chat message that EK wrote in Russian and that was translated for the recipient — disappeared from EK's chat: "It was in Russian and translated as it was the right decision. It disappeared from my chat". That is, the sender sees that the message is gone, while the recipient sees it. Looks like a state-sync bug between sides. 🟠 Medium

⚠️ Translation quality in the meeting

  • Speech translation was delayed: "it was a bit delayed".
  • Some parts of the translation are incorrect: "Some parts of it were a bit off".
  • Not critical, but not "the quality you came for".

✅ What works well

Chat translation (text translation)

  • Two-way message translation works: "On the right you have Russian, and it is automatically translated to English on the left. And the second message is just English on the left, but both of them seem to work".
  • "Chat function seemed to work very well in terms of translation" — overall assessment of chat translation.

Add to contacts from the meeting

  • You can add a participant to contacts directly from the meeting, by clicking on their profile: "when you're in a meeting, you can click on someone's profile and add them to your contacts".

Channels and persistent chats

  • The channel appears for the recipient: "You can see the channel in my channel section. That's worked perfectly".

⚠️ Inconvenient

Timecode What was noted
~07:00 Add to contacts works from the meeting, but NOT on the User page: "I don't see anything to do it in the user page". Inconsistency: the same operation works from one entry point and not from another.

  • Notes in chat (Note editor) — not even mentioned.

🚦 Priorities

  1. 🟠 Message disappears for the sender after translation (~10:00) — needs to be urgently reproduced and understood, whether this is a state-sync bug between sender/recipient view.

  3. 🟡 Add to contacts: unify — should work both from the meeting and from the user page.
