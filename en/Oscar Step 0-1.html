❌ Doesn't work (functional bugs)

Timecode What happened Severity
14:00–14:07 "What is creating an infinite loop?" — when attempting to share screen in the demo room, an infinite visual loop appears (probably the window containing the meeting itself was shared). Oscar immediately stopped sharing. This is a critical UX bug: in Zoom/Meet, in this case a warning is shown or an anti-mirror is used. 🔴 High
07:38–07:52 On the home page (intermind.com) part of the text almost blends into the background — "some of the text is almost the same colour as the background, so it's a bit hard to read". Contrast below WCAG. Reproduced on both desktop and mobile. 🟠 Medium
08:01–08:10 On the Sovereignty page "some of the numbers are not in the center of the circle" — numbers inside circular elements are off-center. Reproduced on iPhone too (19:48–20:03). 🟠 Medium
13:09–13:34 Transcription in demo: "full transcription of what I'm saying in English" is visible, but "not sure if there should be a transcription of what I'm saying in Dutch — it's empty there". Either the second-language transcription is not shown, or the UI doesn't explain what should be displayed and where. 🟠 Medium

⚠️ Inconvenient

Timecode What was noted Type
02:38–02:58 The monthly / yearly toggle on the Pricing page "is maybe slightly smaller, unclear". The toggle is small, not readable at first glance. Micro-UX
07:52–08:01 On the Sovereignty page "some of the text is extremely small" — unreadable font size. On mobile (19:48–20:03) — same thing: "text is a bit small". Typography
18:42–19:00 On iPhone after login via Google, the user lands on the "meetings page" and doesn't understand where they are relative to the landing: "I'm not on the actual forum page of the website where I can try the demo". A jump into the authenticated part without an explicit indicator "you're signed in as X, this is your home". Navigation after login
18:46–19:27 From the same place the way back to demo is not obvious — Oscar ended up manually typing intermind.com in the address bar. Navigation

❓ Unclear

Timecode What was unclear Context
10:21–10:33 On the Demo screen "space between letters on some of the demo mode is a bit strange". Marked it himself as "nitpicking", but perceived it as visual noise that distracts at the start. Demo-CTA typography
11:18 In demo "I see myself on both sides of the screen" — Oscar sees his own video on both the left and the right. Didn't understand if that's intentional (preview + simulated counterpart) or a bug. The UI doesn't explain this. Self-view duplicated
11:34–11:46 Listening to his own translation back in headphones: "I can hear it in my own voice — that is very strange… actually that's pretty cool". At first discomfort from his own synthesized voice, then interest. Worth showing the user in advance what they will hear. Expectations onboarding
16:48–17:14 After exiting the demo and re-entering, transcription started in German (although it had just been Dutch). "Has the language changed?" — unclear why the language switched, whether the product remembers the setting, what it chooses by default. Demo: state persistence
15:00–15:50 When trying to test translation to Russian, Oscar couldn't: "I cannot speak Russian". Translation can't be tested alone — a real conference is needed. Decided to defer to the next step. Demo limitation: solo use

✅ What works well (positive signals)

An important part of the report — confirmation that the happy path isn't broken.

Pricing

  • "Pricing plans clearly sected" — the structure of the plans is readable.
  • "Text is bold, clear, very easy to understand".
  • "Features… how each plan affects what you'll have access to — quite clear, I like the use of tick symbols".

FAQ

  • "Drop-down menus very easy to navigate".
  • "Questions you've chosen are very relevant".
  • "Answers are good, concise".
  • "Before you pick a plan" — allows people to decide for themselves.

Use cases

  • "Easy to navigate", "clear and concise", "symbols aesthetically pleasing".

Landing (intermind.com)

  • "Very clear slogan straight away" ("Stop running meetings in someone else's language").
  • "Very simple, clear description of what the service provides".
  • "Live demo — very clear button, contrast between light blue and dark background makes it easy to find".
  • All feature pages (real-time translation, collaborative workspace, privacy & security) — "opens successfully, no bugs".

Demo (happy path)

  • Permissions popup works cleanly.
  • Translation to Dutch works.
  • Mute — works.
  • "Was vrij eenvoudig te navigeren" — it was fairly simple to navigate.
  • Re-entry into demo — "everything seems pretty much exactly the same, it's loading up again".

Mobile (iPhone, Safari)

  • Landing "properly formatted for iPhone" — responsive works.
  • Google login works.
  • Same UI as on desktop.
