❌ Doesn't work (functional bugs)

Timecode What happened Severity
~04:30 Transcript lag ~5 seconds: "big lag in the transcript, I see, when Oscar speaks, that text and voice come out 5 seconds behind". 5 seconds is a lot, the text arrives no longer at the pace of the conversation. 🔴 High
~03:30 Confirmation of EK 5-6: "Different phrases ended up in the translation, and in the text above" — garbage / random phrases in the translation window. Reproduced independently. 🔴 High
~04:00 Translation voice sometimes "isn't yours, but someone else's" — with "some kind of modest phrase": "Sometimes I heard that it wasn't in your voice, but with other people, with some modest phrase". Voice cloning glitches / substitutes the voice. New finding, wasn't seen with EK. 🔴 High

⚠️ Strange

  • "I'm somehow surprised when I heard that I was speaking Russian" — repeat of the finding about reacting to one's own synthesized voice (was present in both testers in §0–1).

✅ What works

  • Two-way RU ↔︎ EN translation works overall.
  • "It translates very interestingly… because Russian was being spoken in your voice" — voice cloning works (when it doesn't glitch, see above).
  • Source-language indicator in the upper corner — found independently: "In the top side we see the language it's being translated from".

❓ Subtitles not completed

  • "Subtitles button" — Oscar tried to enable it.
  • Transcription button — mentioned.
  • In the end: "I don't know how to check this, so I think we're done with this part". The tester didn't understand how to validate Subtitles separately from Translation/Transcription. The UI doesn't explain how the three buttons differ.

📋 Not covered

  • 17 languages — only RU/EN.
  • Mixed speech in a single phrase (RU + EN interleaved).
  • Fast speech, whisper.
  • Subtitles standalone (didn't figure out).
  • Transcription standalone, download transcript, check a rare language in transcription.
  • State persistence for subtitles/transcription between meetings.

🚦 Priorities

  1. 🔴 Voice cloning substitutes the voice (~04:00) — serious quality bug, new finding.
  2. 🔴 5-second transcript lag (~04:30) — critical for the UX of "simultaneous translation".
  3. 🔴 Hallucinations in the translation window — confirmed independently by Oscar + EK.
  4. 🟠 UI of the three buttons Translation/Subtitles/Transcription — the user doesn't understand the difference. Tooltips or an explanation in Help are needed.
