❌ Doesn't work / strange

Timecode What happened Severity
~10:00 Limit "message too long for translate, maximum is 1000 characters" when pasting a large text from Wikipedia into chat. This is a per-message limit, not an explicit characteristic of the plan. The pricing page promises limits in "10K/200K/3M words" — this is something else (an aggregate per-plan limit). The user doesn't understand the relationship and sees "1000 chars max" as a bug. The message arrives in the original, without translation. 🟠 Medium
~12:00 "There are small bugs in chat when looking at the translation. In some places it's in Russian, in some in English" — the UI is not consistent: the same feed shows different messages in different languages without explicit control. 🟠 Medium

✅ What works

In-meeting chat

  • Two-way message translation works: "the first message split in two, with Russian on the right and automatically translated into English on the left".
  • "Everything works well in this part" (overall assessment of translation in chat).

Chat after the meeting / persistent chat

  • Chat history is preserved: "the chat that opens during the call is the same as during our chat outside the call, it continued".

Creating a channel after adding contacts

  • Confirmation of the nuance from §3: a channel without contacts can't be created, but from a meeting you can add a participant to contacts via clicking the profile.
  • After adding: "now I have a channel with Oscar".

Note Editor ⭐

  • Creating a note from chat works.
  • The note is visible on the other side: "Try to find a note of some kind, if you see it in the chat… Yes, here it is".
  • Deleting a note propagates to both sides: "delete the note… It has disappeared".
  • Translation of the note into the recipient's language works.

❓ Inconvenient (continued)

Timecode What was noted
~07:00 To create a channel, contacts are needed. Contacts are added only from a meeting (via profile). This is "a bit uncomfortable" — a workaround "enter the meeting to add a contact" is required. There should be a direct path "add a contact by email" outside a meeting.

📋 Not covered

  • File attachments in chat (mentioned in passing, but not shown as a test).
  • Emoji in chat.
  • Forwarding messages / copy link / mention.
  • Search in messages.
  • Creating a meeting from chat (new §8 feature) — Oscar mentioned "button for join", but didn't clarify.
  • Notifications about a new message when out of focus.

🚦 Priorities

  1. 🟠 1000-character per-message limit + the mismatch with the pricing promise (10K/200K/3M words): explain to the user what these numbers mean and why there's currently a stop at 1000 chars.
  2. 🟠 Translation inconsistency in the chat feed — one feed, different languages in different bubbles.
  3. 🟡 Add contact outside the meeting — add a direct path.
  4. ✅ Note editor — works exemplarily end-to-end.
