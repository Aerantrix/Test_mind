✅ What works

Screen sharing

  • The sharing itself works between two people (but isn't elaborated on much in this recording).

Laser pointer ⭐

  • "We can paint on a page, me and Oskar… Oscar just painted a smile, so we can paint on the screen with dots… laser walks". The laser pointer works both ways, both participants can "draw" on a single shared screen. This is a rare and pleasant feature for presentations.

Recording start UI

  • Clear path: "To record I need to open the button More and start server recording. So now I see the top bottom that recording started". Button via More menu, status indicator visible.

⚠️ Oddity

  • "We disabled our translation, but I still see under the Oscars that there is button transcribing. Maybe because our language is still different for our accounts" — even when translation is off, the transcribing indicator stays on the other side. Inconsistent state between meeting participants.
